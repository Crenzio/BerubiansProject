Welcome to the Berubian Theater

We're a small community black-box theater that's always on the hunt for new talent and new audience members.

We believe that the primary duty of a community theater is to supports its performs, writers, and directors.

We welcome veterans and newcomers alike. We love helping new actors get started, and helping veteran actors further refine their talents. As a matter of principal, we never charge our actors dues or fees (nor do we make them buy anything, subscribe to anything, or pay us in any other obscure way).

If you're an actor who's interested in performing with us, keep any eye on our Auditions Page, or join our mailing list, to keep abrest of new opportunities to act on our stage.

But we do more than support actors. We also take every opportunity to provide writers, directors, and stand-up comedians with a place to put on a their shows.

If you're a stand-up comedian in need of a place to perform, a writer with a script that needs a home, or a director lookiing for a show to run, ask about Renting Our Space.
