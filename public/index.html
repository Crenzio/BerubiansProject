Welcome to the Berubian Theater

We're a community black-box theater that's always on the hunt for new talent and new audiences.

We believe that the primary duty of a community theater is to support its performs, writers, and directors. And we never take that responsibility lightly.

We welcome veterans and newcomers alike. We love helping new actors get started, and helping veteran actors further refine their talents. As a matter of principal, we never charge our performers dues or fees (nor do we make them pay us in any other way).

If you're an actor who's interested in performing with us, keep any eye on our Auditions Page, join our Mailing List, or follow us on Social Media.

But we do more than support actors. We also take every opportunity to provide writers, directors, and stand-up comedians with a place to put on their shows.

Are you a comedian looking for a place to perform? A director looking for a show to run? A teacher who needs space for a workshop? Or someone looking to host an event? Well, then ask about Renting Our Space.

If you're an aspiring performer looking to sharpen your skills, or an enthusiast who just wants to hang out and do improv, then take a look at our Classes.

Everyone else, take a look at the Shows we have playing.
